- JWT secret loaded from environment variable (no hardcoded secrets)
- Apple Sign-In audience claim validated against app bundle ID
- Avatar upload size limited to 8 MB
- Username conflict returns 409 instead of 500
- Drive ownership enforced: users can only access their own drives
