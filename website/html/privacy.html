Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 4, 2026

FastTrack ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use the FastTrack iOS application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information. When you sign in with Apple or Google, we receive a unique identifier, and optionally your email address and display name, depending on the permissions you grant.

Drive Data. When you record a drive, we collect GPS coordinates, speed, acceleration, G-force measurements, timestamps, and other sensor readings. This data is associated with your account and stored on our servers.

Location Data. FastTrack uses your device's GPS to track your route and measure performance metrics during active drive recording sessions. Location data is only collected while you have an active recording session running. We do not collect location data in the background.

Device & Usage Data. We may collect basic device information (device model, OS version, app version) and crash/error reports to improve app stability.

Profile Information. You may optionally provide a username, profile photo, country, and vehicle information. This information may be visible to other FastTrack users on leaderboards and public profiles.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the FastTrack service
  • To calculate and display performance metrics (0–60, quarter-mile, G-forces, etc.)
  • To power leaderboards and social features
  • To sync your data across devices
  • To diagnose crashes and fix bugs
  • To communicate service updates (if you provide an email address)

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in the following circumstances:

  • Public profile & leaderboards: Your username, vehicle, and performance stats may be visible to other users if you participate in leaderboards or have a public profile.
  • Service providers: We use third-party services for infrastructure (cloud hosting) and authentication (Apple Sign In, Google Sign In). These providers process data only as necessary to provide their services.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of FastTrack and its users.

4. Authentication Providers

FastTrack supports sign-in via Apple Sign In and Google Sign In. When you use these services, you are subject to Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies in addition to this one. We only receive the information these providers share with us based on your authorization.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account and drive data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the address below. Drive data you delete is removed from our servers within 30 days.

6. Security

Authentication tokens are stored in the iOS Keychain (not in iCloud-synced storage). Data in transit is encrypted via TLS. We use industry-standard practices to protect your data, but no system is perfectly secure.

7. Children's Privacy

FastTrack is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or 16 in some jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or export your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
